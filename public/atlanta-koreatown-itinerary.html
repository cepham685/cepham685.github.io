Good to know

  • Reserve now: Jak Omakase (Fri 7:30 PM) seats just a handful — book ahead. Gopchang spots get a wait on Sat nights too.
  • Saturday is packed: soup → mall → Korean-Chinese lunch → Activate is a lot before a 7 PM gopchang dinner. Going to Moo Bong Ri at 9 and keeping lunch a shared graze makes it fit; drop the mall or lunch if it feels rushed.
  • July 4 fireworks fork: easy free show at Newtown Park after dinner, or the marquee Stone Mountain show if you move dinner earlier.
  • Heat: Atlanta in July is hot & humid — push hikes and outdoor walks to early morning.
  • Weather: shown highs/lows are typical for early July — forecasts this far out shift, so check again the week of.
  • Home base: nearly every food stop is within ~15 min of Duluth; Activate, Stone Mountain & the fireworks are the longer hops.
